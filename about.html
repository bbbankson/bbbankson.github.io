ABOUT CATERING RECIPES MEDIA CONTACT

Hi! I'm a private chef and caterer (currently) located in Seattle.

My food and cooking process is inspired by the ephemera of seasons, which concretely just means that (among other things) I like to cook vegetables, heirloom beans, offal and whole grains with as many herbs, citrus, and flavorful fats as I can appropriately combine in a dish. I am in awe of the farmers, winemakers, and general land stewards around me in the Pacific Northwest: I view cooking as my way of honoring those meaningful + regenerative relationships with the land that we live on.

I would love to work with you on your next culinary event, whatever the occasion: birthday, anniversary, wedding, just a fun weeknight! Feel free to contact me through email or on Instagram and we can come up with a bespoke menu that reflects your preferences and my expertise - I can't wait! Click through the images on the “catering” tab above for some examples of previous menus and photos of my food. Thanks for visiting!

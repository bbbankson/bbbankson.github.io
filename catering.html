catering & private chef — As a self-taught cook, my professional cooking experience has been broad and eclectic — I love to say yes to food! I’ve done tasting menus for two people and coursed dinners for 14, but also day-to-day meal services, workplace receptions for 60, and wedding meals for 180. I have a deep interest in wine (with particular knowledge of low intervention producers from the West Coast, New England, and the Loire Valley), amaro, and low/no-proof beverages, and enjoy curating beverage selections for any kind of event. I’m currently located in Seattle but love to travel around the US for events!
